More About Me

My name is Nayeli Alejandra Pérez Torres. I was born and raised in El Paso, TX and moved to San Antonio to attend Trinity University. I graduated in 2016 and have been working as a designer since. I also do freelance photography and personal photography work. I enjoy observing, analyzing, and talking about both art and design. Outside of art and design, I enjoy playing and watching soccer (Visca Barça!) and playing the guitar and piano.
